The Quest to Defeat Medusa

Stage Details
1. Finding the Graeae Perseus stole their single eye to force them to reveal Medusa's location.
2. Receiving Divine Aid Hermes and Athena gave Perseus winged sandals, a shield, and a sword.
3. Beheading Medusa Using the shield to avoid her gaze, Perseus beheaded Medusa while she slept.
