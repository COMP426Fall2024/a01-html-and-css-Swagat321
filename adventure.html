The Quest to Defeat Medusa

Stage Details
1. Finding the Graeae Perseus stole their single eye to force them to reveal Medusa's location.
2. Receiving Divine Aid Hermes and Athena gave Perseus winged sandals, a shield, and a sword.
3. Beheading Medusa Using the shield to avoid her gaze, Perseus beheaded Medusa while she slept.
4. Escape from the Gorgons After beheading Medusa, Perseus flew away using his winged sandals to escape from Medusa's sisters, Stheno and Euryale.
5. Saving Andromeda On his way back, Perseus encountered Andromeda, who was chained to a rock as a sacrifice to a sea monster. Perseus saved her by slaying the beast.
6. Returning to Seriphos Perseus returned to the island of Seriphos, where he used Medusa's head to turn King Polydectes to stone, ending his reign of terror.
7. Fulfillment of Prophecy Perseus later accidentally fulfilled a prophecy by killing his grandfather, King Acrisius, with a discus during a sports competition.
